About Us

Feline Café is a coffee shop for cat lovers. Whether you are looking for a calm space to unwind, you want to help cats in need or even if you're thinking of adopting. You'll love the cozy space where you can interact with these lovely rescue cats. It is a calm and happy space where you can get some purr-therapy and potentially find your new family member.

We are a cause-focused business, which means that our business operates in order to support the cats that we foster. With the profits, we are able to pay for housing, food and supplies for these cats that are looking for their forever home.

Using a cat café business model, we support the rescue and their work. Our platform provides them with a full-time foster home where we house the cats, as well as fundraising efforts to pay for their care.

Adopt?

Interested in adopting? Here's how:

  1. Fall in love. Visit the cats in our cafe or choose from the list. We have posters of all the available cats posted in the entrance of our café.
  2. Apply online. Once you have chosen a cat you'd like to adopt, you can apply at the bottom of the page.
  3. Approval. The rescue will be in contact with you to process the application and confirm that the application has been approved.
  4. Payment. The adoption fee is sent directly to the rescue by e-transfer. All money goes to the rescue, not to Feline Café.
  5. Schedule a pickup. Once payment is confirmed, schedule an appointment to pick up your cat by emailing Feline Café.
  6. Prepare your home. Have all toys, litter and food supplies ready before you pick up your cat.
  7. Pick up your new family member. We will walk you through the adoption contract and paperwork, as well as take a photo with your cat for the rescue's website. Please bring a carrier with a blanket or pillow inside to bring your cat home with you.
